# Create a comprehensive Contact page

A clear and accessible contact page is a major trust signal for E-E-A-T and provides essential support for users, improving overall site credibility.

## Quick Reference

- Provide multiple contact methods such as a functional email address, phone number, or contact form
- Include a physical address or map if applicable to establish local presence and trust
- Ensure the contact page is easily discoverable from any page on the site

## Check

Verify that the website has a dedicated Contact page with multiple, working contact methods.

## Fix

Create a `/contact` page and add a contact form, email address, and other relevant contact information.

## Explain

Explain why having a verifiable contact page is a critical factor for business legitimacy and search rankings.
